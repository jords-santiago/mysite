In a world full of whys, say "why not?". In a world full of can not's, say "can do". In a world full of problems, we might as well solve them.

As my previous company closed down during the COVID-19 global pandemic, I took time off to learn new things that I was not able to while working.

During that time off, I created this site as an application of the online courses I have taken and what I learned professionally. It is here that I am able to gather some of my personal projects. Obviously, the site itself is a project on its own.

Up to its closure, I worked as an R&D Engineer at Nokia Manila Technology Center with a position of Senior Specialist. I maintained a test tool software used for performance testing of Nokia's controller products. I was also a Fault Coordinator where I monitored bug reports.

Before that, I was previously a Senior Hardware Engineer at Canon Information Technologies Philippines, Inc. where I worked as a Design Verification Engineer. I had experience as a Configuration Management Lead, Quality Assurance Lead and Technical Lead.

I graduated cum laude with a Bachelor of Science in Electronics and Communications Engineering degree from the University of the Philippines. The education and experience I had there got me started in problem solving and critical/creative thinking, skills that I still use to this day.
